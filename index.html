About

My favorite hobby is sketching realistic objects or people. When I am bored, I'll always practice sketching on something. Sketching may be very satisfying that you'd probably get deeply interested in doing so. However, not only does it interest you, but it also flexes the muscles on your arm. Even though sketching provides these benefits, my reason for sketching, is because it just lets me have fun. I am not judged for my sketches, and even if I'm a beginner at sketching, I still do it, and it makes me feel good. Sketching can help us to relax, exercise our muscles, and let us have fun.
